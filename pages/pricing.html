One credit = one AI generation. Simple art and lore prompts typically cost 1–3 credits. Heavy tasks like Meshy 3D rigging or Kling trailer renders cost 5–15 credits. You can always see the credit cost before confirming a generation. Credits reset monthly and don't roll over.

Project Memory is a locked context object that every tool in Gamesfield reads from. It stores your art style, colour palette, character roster, world lore rules, and world name. Every generation stays consistent without you re-pasting prompts. Think of it as a game bible your AI tools actually listen to.
